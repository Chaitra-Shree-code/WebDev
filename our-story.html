Our Story

Shree Bakery was born from a simple idea — to spread joy through the art of baking. What started as a humble home kitchen with just a few cherished recipes has grown into a beloved community bakery, built on love, passion, and the warmth of togetherness.

Every treat we create is a reflection of our journey — from our founder’s first chocolate cake baked for a friend’s birthday, to the bustling mornings where ovens hum with activity and the aroma of fresh pastries fills the air.

From Our Oven to Your Heart

Over the years, we’ve learned that it’s not just about flour, sugar, and butter — it’s about the memories we make. Whether it’s a birthday, a wedding, or just your morning coffee stop, we’re honored to be part of your everyday moments.

At Shree Bakery, baking is more than a skill — it's our way of saying "You matter."

“Each loaf has a story. Each cake carries a celebration. And each cookie is a hug in disguise.”
